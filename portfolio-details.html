Eventoria - The event Managment System

Project information

  • Category: Web Development
  • Client: G.T.U.
  • Project date: 01 March, 2022
  • Project URL: www.Eventoria.in

Eventoria is the Event Managment System in which all Universities or college can arrange their inter-college, state-level or other Events/compitition very easily. and all the student can easily take part on those events. Students and Organizer will get their own dedicated dashboard.
